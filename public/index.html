Hook (first 2 seconds)

You open on a locked-off shot of yourself sitting down, reaching to adjust the camera. Two full seconds pass before you say a word — a stranger has already flicked past.

FIX →Cut those two seconds. Open mid-sentence on "nobody tells you this about your first year" with the camera already rolling.

Audio hook

Silent for the first second, then a low "um, ok so." Nothing in the sound reaches out and grabs the ear in the window that decides everything.

FIX →Start on a trending sound sting under frame one, or lead with the loudest, most confident line you have.
